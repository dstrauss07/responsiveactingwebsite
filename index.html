Sandra Patton is a professional film, theater and voiceover actress. She is equally adept at portraying the brooding anti-hero with a tattered past, or the crazy neighbor with a heart of gold.

Recently she has written, directed and produced several short films with her production company Doggins Brothers Presents. She has also been a cast member at The Basement Theater and Onion Man Productions, along with acting in several indie film projects.

Sandra Patton is represented by Evolution Talent Agency for Commmercial and Theatrical Film.
